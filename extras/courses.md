# Great Courses

This is a list of high-quality courses that, for one reason or another, didn't make it into the curriculum.
The most common reasons are that the course isn't available often enough,
or that there was an alternative that fit better into the curriculum.

## Sociological Theory

Courses | Duration | Effort
:-- | :--: | :--:
[Foundations of Modern Social Theory (Open Yale Courses)](https://oyc.yale.edu/sociology/socy-151) | 24 lectures | 2-3 hours/week
[Classical Sociological Theory (Coursera - University of Amsterdam)](https://www.coursera.org/learn/classical-sociological-theory) | 7 weeks | 3-5 hours/week
